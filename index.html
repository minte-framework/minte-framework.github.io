About

MINTE is an integration framework that relies on both: knowledge stated in RDF vocabularies and semantic similarity measures to merge heterogenous data into a consolidated RDF graph. MINTE follows a two-fold approach to solve the problem of integrating RDF graphs. In the first step, MINTE implements a 1-1 weighted perfect matching algorithm to identify semantically equivalent RDF entities in different graphs. Then, MINTE relies on different fusion policies to merge triples from these semantically equivalent RDF entities.
